About Us

The official website of "BSNL EMPLOYEES AND CITIZENS HOUSE BUILDING CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETY LTD" for buying "BSNL Society Plots" near International Airport, Devanahalli, Bangalore. The BSNL Housing Society is registered under the co-operative society's act with the Registration Number HSG-2/12/HHS/46469/2014-15.

BSNL Housing Society’s Layout is formed strictly under adhering to the rules and regulations of the Registered Co-Operative Society (RCS) and Bangalore International Airport Area Planning Authority (BIAAPA) norms and specifications, hence every detail of the society will be monitored by the Authorised Departments.
